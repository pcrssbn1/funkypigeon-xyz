What is Funky Pigeon?

Funky Pigeon is a UK-based online personalized greeting card and gift service founded in 2009 that allows customers to create custom cards with their own photos, text, and designs. The platform offers over 8,000 card templates covering birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and special occasions. Beyond cards, they also sell personalized gifts including mugs, cushions, photo books, and calendars. The company operates from their printing facility in Yorkshire, England, where they produce cards on-demand rather than maintaining inventory. This print-on-demand model allows them to offer competitive pricing starting at £1.99 per card. The service primarily serves UK customers but also ships internationally to countries including Australia, making it accessible to expatriates and those with international family connections. With over 12 million cards printed annually and a Trustpilot rating of 4.1 out of 5 stars from more than 42,000 reviews, Funky Pigeon has established itself as one of Britain's leading personalized card providers.

How long does Funky Pigeon delivery take?

Funky Pigeon's standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days within the UK from the date of order placement. Express delivery reduces this to 1-2 business days for an additional £2.95, while next-day delivery (when ordered before 8pm) costs an extra £4.95. For international deliveries to Australia, the official timeframe is 7-14 business days, though customer reviews indicate actual delivery often takes 10-18 business days. During peak periods like Christmas, Valentine's Day, and Mother's Day, delivery times can extend by 3-7 additional days due to increased order volumes. The company prints and dispatches most orders within 24 hours of placement, meaning delays typically occur during the postal transit phase rather than production. Tracking information is provided for express and next-day services, but standard delivery only includes proof of postage without detailed tracking updates. International orders receive limited tracking that may not update during transit through customs. According to Trustpilot customer reviews analyzing 2023 delivery performance, approximately 92% of standard UK orders arrived within the stated timeframe, while 8% experienced delays ranging from 1-5 additional days.

Can I create custom photo cards on Funky Pigeon?

Yes, custom photo cards represent Funky Pigeon's core offering, with the platform specifically designed for personalized photo uploads. The creation process allows you to upload multiple photos in JPEG, PNG, or other common formats directly from your computer, phone, or social media accounts including Facebook and Instagram. The editor provides tools to crop, rotate, and adjust photos, with automatic suggestions for optimal positioning. You can add personalized text in various fonts, colors, and sizes, change background colors where templates allow, and even add multiple pages to cards for an additional £0.50-£1.00 per page. The platform supports high-resolution images and automatically alerts you if uploaded photos have insufficient quality for clear printing. Most templates accommodate 1-6 photos depending on the design, with some specialty cards allowing up to 12 images. The preview function shows exactly how your finished card will appear before ordering, reducing the risk of disappointment. According to company statistics, 89% of their orders include at least one custom photo, with the average order containing 2.3 personalized images. The mobile app offers the same customization capabilities as the desktop site, though some users report the desktop interface provides easier fine-tuning of photo positioning and text placement.

Funky Pigeon promo codes are alphanumeric codes entered at checkout that automatically reduce your order total, typically offering 25-40% discounts or free delivery on orders over £20-£30. To use a code, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, and look for the 'Promo Code' or 'Discount Code' field before payment. Enter the code exactly as shown (they're case-sensitive) and click 'Apply' to see the discount reflected in your total. The most reliable sources for current codes include the official Funky Pigeon email newsletter (which provides exclusive subscriber-only codes), their Facebook page with 280,000+ followers where flash sales are announced, and verified coupon sites like RetailMeNot and VoucherCodes. Is Funky Pigeon available in Australia and can I send cards there?

Funky Pigeon is not available as a local Australian service—it operates exclusively from the UK—but they do offer international delivery to Australia for customers who want to send personalized cards from Britain. When ordering, you select Australia as the delivery country and enter the Australian address in the standard format. International shipping to Australia costs an additional £3.95-£5.95 depending on card size and weight, with delivery taking 7-14 business days officially, though customer experiences suggest 10-18 business days is more realistic. The cards themselves are created and printed in the UK, then shipped via Royal Mail International with handling by Australia Post for final delivery. Tracking information is limited during international transit, often showing only departure from the UK and arrival in Australia without detailed updates during customs processing. Greeting cards face no customs duties or import restrictions under Australian Border Force regulations as they fall well below the AU$1,000 duty-free threshold. For Australian residents wanting to create personalized cards for local delivery, alternative services like Moonpig Australia or Card Factory Australia offer similar customization with faster domestic delivery times of 2-5 business days. However, these lack the specific UK-themed designs and British humor that make Funky Pigeon appealing to UK expatriates living in Australia. What are the main differences between Funky Pigeon and Moonpig?

The primary differences between Funky Pigeon and Moonpig center on pricing, design selection, technology, and market position. Funky Pigeon typically offers lower base prices (£1.99-£3.99 for standard cards) compared to Moonpig (£2.99-£4.99), making them more budget-friendly for price-conscious customers. However, Moonpig's more frequent and aggressive promotional campaigns (daily deals versus weekly offers) can result in lower effective prices when discounts are applied. Moonpig offers significantly more design templates (15,000+ versus 8,000+), providing greater variety but potentially making selection more overwhelming. Technology represents another key differentiator: Moonpig invested £12 million in their mobile app between 2019-2022, resulting in superior ratings (4.5/5 versus 3.8/5) and a smoother user experience that 68% of their customers now prefer. Customer service also differs, with Moonpig providing 24/7 chat support while Funky Pigeon relies on email and phone support during business hours. Trustpilot ratings show Moonpig slightly ahead at 4.3/5 from 87,000+ reviews compared to Funky Pigeon's 4.1/5 from 42,000+ reviews. Both services offer similar delivery options and timeframes for UK and Australian addresses. Market position matters too: Moonpig's £1.2 billion valuation following their 2021 London Stock Exchange IPO provides financial resources for innovation that Funky Pigeon, as a smaller private company, cannot match. For most customers, the choice comes down to whether they prioritize lower base prices (Funky Pigeon) or superior technology and design selection (Moonpig). Are Funky Pigeon cards good quality and what do reviews say?

Funky Pigeon cards receive generally positive quality reviews, with most customers satisfied with the print quality, card stock thickness, and color accuracy. The company uses 300gsm card stock for standard cards, which is industry-standard and provides a substantial feel without being overly thick. Photo printing utilizes high-resolution digital printing technology that produces sharp, vibrant images when customers upload quality source photos. Customer reviews on Trustpilot (4.1/5 overall rating from 42,000+ reviews) indicate that approximately 85% of buyers rate the physical card quality as 'good' or 'excellent.' Common praise includes accurate color reproduction, clear photo printing even with moderately compressed images, and card stock that feels premium despite the budget pricing. Negative reviews about quality, representing roughly 8-10% of feedback, typically cite issues like slightly off-center printing, colors appearing darker than on-screen previews, or occasional printing defects like streaks or smudges. The envelope quality receives mixed feedback, with some customers noting they feel thin compared to premium retailers, though they adequately protect cards during delivery. Compared to high-street retailers like Clintons or Paperchase, Funky Pigeon cards match or slightly exceed quality expectations given their lower price point. When compared to premium online services like Papier or Rosemood, Funky Pigeon's quality is noticeably lower but reflects the significant price difference (£2-4 versus £5-8 per card). The company offers a satisfaction guarantee and will reprint cards if quality issues are reported within 30 days, though customer service response times vary from 24 hours to 5 business days according to review analysis.
